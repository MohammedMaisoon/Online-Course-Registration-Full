Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend using the live chat feature available on our platform during operating hours.

How can I request a refund?

Refund requests should be submitted through your account dashboard under "Purchases" or by contacting our support team at help@eduplatform.com. Please review our refund policy for eligibility criteria.

Can I become an instructor on the platform?

Yes! We welcome expert instructors in various fields. To apply, visit the "Become an Instructor" page from our main menu or contact us at partners@eduplatform.com for more information about the application process.

Do you offer corporate training solutions?

We offer customized learning solutions for businesses of all sizes. Please contact our business team at business@eduplatform.com to discuss your organization's specific training needs and how we can help.
